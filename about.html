About Apex

Family-owned. Built for the committed. Coming to Vermilion, Ohio.

Our Story

Built from a passion for the craft.

Apex Strength Club is a family-owned strength and fitness facility coming to Vermilion, Ohio. Built from a passion for bodybuilding, healthy living, and serious training, Apex is designed to give the community a gym experience unlike anything currently available in the area.

From premium equipment and an electric atmosphere to recovery amenities, a smoothie bar, supplements, Apex merchandise, and a dedicated posing room, every detail is being built with purpose. The project is led by local owner John Decker, with Apex taking shape on a three-acre site at 4345 Liberty Avenue.

Built In Vermilion

Built in Vermilion. Built for the committed.

Apex is being built for lifters, bodybuilders, athletes, and health-focused locals who want a cleaner, more polished, better-equipped gym experience — without driving to a larger market.
