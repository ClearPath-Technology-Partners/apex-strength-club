Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This page explains what information Apex Strength Club collects through this website and, once membership sign-ups open, through our membership and facility-access program, and how that information is used. This is a plain-language summary, not a substitute for formal legal advice.

What We Collect

  • Site analytics: we use Vercel Web Analytics, which measures visits using an anonymous, daily-rotating hash rather than cookies or personal identifiers.
  • Email signup & contact form: if you join our email list or contact us, we collect the name, email address, and any message you provide so we can send updates and respond to you.
  • Membership information (once enrollment opens): if you sign up for a membership, we collect the information on your Membership Agreement, such as your name, address, phone number, date of birth, emergency contact, and signed waiver.
  • Payment information (once enrollment opens): membership dues are processed by a third-party payment processor. We do not store your full card or bank account number on our own systems.
  • Facility access data (once open): our 24/7 access system logs check-in times through your member app or key fob for security and billing purposes.

How We Use It

Information collected is used to operate this website and our facility, administer memberships and billing, respond to inquiries, maintain facility security, and send opt-in updates about Apex Strength Club's opening and offers. We do not sell your personal information.

Third Parties

We share information only with service providers who help us operate: Vercel (website hosting and analytics), our email provider, and, once launched, our payment processor and 24/7 access-control app vendor. These providers may only use your information to perform services for us, not for their own marketing.

Data Retention

We keep contact and membership information for as long as needed to provide our services, comply with legal or tax obligations, and resolve disputes, after which it is deleted or anonymized.

Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through the Site. Membership by minors is subject to the parental/guardian consent terms in our Membership Agreement.

Your Choices & Rights

You can ask to see, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you, or opt out of marketing emails at any time, by contacting us at apexstrengthclub00@gmail.com. If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what personal information we collect and to request deletion; we do not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We will update this page as our services expand, particularly once membership sign-up and facility access go live, and will update the "Last updated" date above when we do.

Questions

If you have questions about this policy, reach out via our Contact page.
