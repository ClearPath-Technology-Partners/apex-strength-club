This page explains what information the Apex Strength Club website collects and how it's used. This is a plain-language summary, not a substitute for formal legal advice.

What We Collect

Information collected is used only to operate this website, respond to inquiries, and send opt-in updates about Apex Strength Club's opening and offers. We do not sell your information.
